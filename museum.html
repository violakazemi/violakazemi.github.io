Project Overview

The Museum Planning Companion is a digital tool designed to bridge the gap between educational goals and logistical reality. By integrating curriculum-linked information with accessibility-aware tools, it transforms the field trip experience for educators.

The Problem

Tara, an elementary school teacher managing large student groups, requires a centralized, transparent booking and logistical planning tool because the current fragmented process—riddled with opaque pricing and hidden operational details—forces her to engage in exhausting manual coordination, leaving her unable to focus on the educational experience for her students.

The Goal

Our Museum Planning Companion app will simplify the field trip booking and coordination process for educators by integrating curriculum-linked exhibit information with real-time, accessibility-aware logistical tools, which will be measured by a reduction in time spent on booking tasks and an increase in user confidence regarding group accessibility needs.
