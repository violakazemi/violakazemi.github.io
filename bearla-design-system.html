08

Usage Principles

ElementRule
MaroonReserved for one primary CTA per view and headline word-emphasis. Never use as a large background outside the Stories module.
GoldMetallic/material cues only — icons, rings, stars, section eyebrows. Not used for text links or buttons.
Serif italicsEmphasize one or two words per headline max — the technique loses power if overused.
Numbering (01/02/03)Reserved for genuine sequences (the 3-step process). Don't apply to non-sequential content.
CornersSharp, 2–4px radius throughout — no pill buttons, no heavy rounding. Circles are reserved for material swatches and the hero locket motif.
DividersSmall centered gold flourish (◆ or hairline + dot) under section eyebrows, used sparingly.
Focus blue (Dodger Blue)System-reserved only: keyboard focus rings, form validation, checkbox/radio checked states. Never used decoratively — its high saturation exists to stand out from the warm palette on purpose.
